About Parma575

Community radio, preparedness, public service, and friendly conversation.

Our Mission

Parma575 promotes responsible GMRS operation, emergency preparedness, radio education, and community connection across Northeast Ohio.

Who We Are

Members include hobbyists, families, storm spotters, off-road users, emergency communicators, and new radio operators.

Why Join?

Meet local operators, learn about radio equipment, check into weekly nets, and participate in club events.
