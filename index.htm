About Parma575

Our Mission

Parma575 exists to promote responsible GMRS operation, community service, emergency preparedness, and friendly conversation across Cuyahoga county and surrounding counties.

Who We Are

Our members include hobbyists, off-road enthusiasts, storm spotters, families, and new radio operators interested in reliable local communications.

Why Join?

Meet fellow operators and learn about radio equipment.
